Hvordan afinstallerer jeg denne app?
Gå til Indstillinger > Sikkerhed > Enhedsadministratorer og disable AcDisplay. You can now afinstallere AcDisplay normalt.

Hvordan afviser jeg permanent underretningerne for aktiv tilstand og låseskærmen?
The notification is necessary to ensure AcDisplay isn't killed by the system. However, to hide it safely (sending test notification won't function anymore!) åbne app info under Indstillinger > Apps og fjerne markeringen ved Vis meddelelser.

When will the secure lockscreen (pattern/PIN) be implemented?
It is not possible to create a custom, secure lockscreen for Android. Other apps that have this feature only pretend to be secure, but in the real world there's always a way to bypass them.
