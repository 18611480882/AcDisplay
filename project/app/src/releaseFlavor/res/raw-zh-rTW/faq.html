我怎麼移除這個應用程式？
去設置 > 安全 > 裝置管理員，然後 disable AcDisplay. You can now 移除 AcDisplay。

要如何隱藏狀態列上的主動模式通知或螢幕鎖通知
The notification is necessary to ensure AcDisplay isn't killed by the system. However, to hide it safely (sending test notification won't function anymore!) 啟動應用程式資訊 後取消選擇顯示通知。
