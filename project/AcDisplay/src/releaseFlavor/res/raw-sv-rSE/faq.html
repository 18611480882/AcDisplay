Hur kan jag avinstallera denna app?
Gå till inställningar > säkerhet > enhets administratörer och avmarkera AcDisplay. Du kan nu avinstallera AcDisplay normalt.

How to the permanent notifications of active mode or the lockscreen?
The notification is necessary to ensure AcDisplay isn't killed by the system. However, to hide it safely (sending test notification won't function anymore!) launch app info and disable showing notifications.

How to use the blacklist?
To use the blacklist, you must enable the app and configure it as well. To enable it, simply click the switch button. To configure it for the app, click the item you want to configure.
